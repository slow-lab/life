Bike generator workshop

Barcelona, June 2023

Hosted together with Low-tech Magazine, this workshop was the occasion to open conversations about our use of resources and the alternatives we can experiment with.

We started with a talk about self-production of energy and followed with a practical part with electricity basics and the creation of a bike-generator.

Working in teams, participants made control panels and hacked electrical devices to power them with the bike direct current.
